Automatic water level controller using IC 555 Timer:-

An automatic water level controller using the IC 555 timer is a circuit that monitors and maintains water levels in a tank. It uses the 555 timer in bistable mode to control the water pump, switching it on when the water level is low and off when it reaches the desired level. Sensors detect the water level and trigger the 555 timer to automate the pump operation.
